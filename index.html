SKKU Courses for UiO IFI Exchange Students

This page lists selected courses at SKKU that are relevant for UiO IFI exchange students. Each course links to the official course description.

Full-time studies at SKKU are 15 credits for students at bachelor's level and 9 credits for students at master's level.

For a bachelor's student, we recommend you to take major related courses for 9 credits, 3 for the math courses, and 3 with your own interest.

For a master's student, we recommend you to take major related courses for 9 credits.

For more information, visit UiO's SKKU exchange page.

Disclaimer: This information is intended for planning purposes. Exact course availability and details may change based on SKKU's official course offerings.
